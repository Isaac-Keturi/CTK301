Isaac spans different moods and feels in his music. These 3 picks are curated to display the different subgeneras he works in.

Ketuzi · Ketuzi - Wario Dies

Wario Dies was an experiment to just get a song finished from start to end and released. The overall sounds and design of the song are pretty simple yet it is that simplicity that some people enjoy. Some people just want to hear a grimey bass line.

Ketuzi · Ketuzi - Subtlety

This is the most recent release and is quite the switch up from his other songs. Subtletly was an attempt to focus on beatmaking and melody.

Ketuzi · Ketuzi - The Four Giants (Calling the Four Giants remix)

The Four Giants remix is his most proud work as it is the most well mixed track he has finished. With shifts in intensity and a synthline that grabs you, The Four Giants hits the mark.
